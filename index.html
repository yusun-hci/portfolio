3D Printing & Sketching with hardware

ElecTwin

ElecTwin is a smart electronics set that can sense the physical world and generate the data of its digital twin. All of the electronics elements are designed in 3D modelling software and 3D printed. It can be well applied in the electronics education for children due to its safe and easy-to-use characteristics like lego-block.

You can have multiple pages in your project. Find the menu icon at the top left corner. Move your cursor over it to open the main menu. Click on the Pages tab to manage your pages.
